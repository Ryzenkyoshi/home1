ABOUT US

more information about us!

Step into Khay's Burger, where each burger tells a delicious story! Our patties are grilled to perfection,
the buns are toasted to golden perfection, and every topping is a flavor-packed delight. From the first bite to the last, you'll experience a burger like never before. Join us at Khay's Burger
and discover why our burgers are more than just a meal – they're an unforgettable experience!

At first our business was just a small diner, and we named it Khay's Burger House, after the changes that we've done. And that's were the Khay's Burger House were establishted, it was way back in 2016. Our first menu was palabok, pansit, lugaw, and any kind of meryenda. Little by little we added burgers to our menu and combined it with chips, carbonara and etc.

Our business thrived because of the combos we added to our menu and it became more and more popular to the students. Khay's Burger House also started the trend of the different color of the buns.
Our current popular food in our menu is chicken burger.

But Khay's Burger is more than just a place to eat. It's a place to gather, to connect, and to create memories.
Whether you're here for a quick bite during your lunch break or a leisurely dinner with friends and family, our cozy ambiance and friendly staff make you feel right at home.
